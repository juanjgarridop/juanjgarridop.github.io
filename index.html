About

Juan José is from Lima, Peru. He is a third-year PhD student in Bilingualism and Second Language Acquisition at Rutgers University. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Education with a concentration in English Language Teaching from Universidad Nacional Federico Villarreal (Lima, Peru). He obtained a master’s degree in Spanish from Auburn University. His ample experience teaching both English and Spanish as a second language inspired his interest in how L2 learners and bilinguals process, perceive, and acquire language.

His research interests include: second language acquisition, bilingualism, phonetics and phonology, speech processing, spoken word recognition, predictive processing, among others.
